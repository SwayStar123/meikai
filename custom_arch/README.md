### 明快 (meikai/míngkuài)
##### Meanings
1. clear and concise;
2. easy to understand;
---

An autoencoder utilizing a hourglass transformer architecture, utilizing VA-VAE and EQ-VAE regularization techniques to make the latent space highly diffusable.
